About Us

About Us

We are team of students from FSKTM dedicated to raise cancer awareness among the communities of University of Malaya. We had deploy this Cancer Awareness website using HTML, CSS and Javascript. By implementing this website, we hope to spread the importance of early detections of cancer and build a community where everyone can help those who’s themselves or their love ones are suffering from cancer.

This project was founded on 13 September 2018 and it is one of our course assignment. We would like to thank all of our team members who contributed their time and worked very hard to make this project successful. We would also like to take this opportunity to express our sincere appreciation to Dr. Chiam Yin Kia, our lecturer of this course. She put a lots of efforts on guiding us with useful comments on how we could make this project better and helped us when we encountered problems during the development of this project.

On 12 October 2018, we launched the cancer awareness website. It has been a wonderful journey since then, with memorable events along the way with our development team, customization of webpage, enhancement on user interface and mobile apps development. But the most valuable moment for us is when we received positive feedback from our users, satisfactions of our customers. Many of users love this community and involved themselves into it. We, as the project team, would like to shout-out to every users on this site, thank you for involving yourself in this community, we could not make it without your support.
